Quick notes for beta-testers

0. This product is being developed to help people make better use and get more joy out of their space. Thank you for being one of the firsts to try it out.

1. For now you will have a much better experience on desktop rather than mobile

2. Click on any red bar to modifiy the dimensions. Use the controllers on the right to remove and add modules

3. Press 'a' and click on a module to move it around. Press Shift + 'a' to move and duplicate

4. If you find bugs, reload the page. Let me know if you can reproduce it

5. If you get to a design you like, click "saveShelf" on the right and send me the file. I am hoping to start manufacturing pre-orders from Santa Cruz in the next few months
